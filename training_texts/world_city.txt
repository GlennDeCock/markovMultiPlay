the city is empty and has always been empty in the way that matters
the streets are clean because no one has been here to make them dirty
the windows are intact and reflect nothing because there is nothing moving to reflect

the buildings stand at the correct distances from each other
the pavements are the correct width for pedestrians if there were pedestrians

the fountain runs

behind the sign there are shelves with things on them arranged by category
the categories are correct and the things are in the correct categories

the library is also open

it has been on

the sky is the same sky as always with clouds of the usual kind moving at the usual speed
no birds

the lobby has a desk and behind the desk there are keys on hooks

the square has cobblestones and a drain in the middle
the drain works

the parking garage has fluorescent lights that flicker in the third row
they have flickered in the third row for a long time
the rest of the lights are steady

the numbers go up to four hundred and twelve

the houses have different coloured doors which makes them not identical in the way that matters for streets
behind the doors the furniture is in the rooms it belongs in

the clock on the wall of the house three doors down shows a time that stopped being correct when the power went out
but the power going out was not a dramatic event it simply went out

the films were showing when the letters were put up

the courtyard has a single tree
the tree is the right tree for this climate and this amount of light

the city continues in all directions from here in the same way
more streets more buildings more things in their correct places

nothing is open that was not already open
