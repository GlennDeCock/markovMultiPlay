a corridor made of polite misunderstandings led to a room full of almost-answers
the almost-answers were arranged alphabetically which made them easier to ignore
ignoring things takes a surprising amount of effort said the room to the corridor
the corridor did not reply because it was busy being somewhere else at both ends
at both ends of the corridor stood two people who had arrived from different directions and were now confused about which direction was which
direction is mostly a suggestion said one of them who had once been a compass
being a compass is a lot of responsibility especially on cloudy days
on cloudy days the suggestion goes vague and everything becomes approximately north
approximately north is where most lost things end up eventually
eventually is a place that is always further away than it appears said the former compass
the other person at the other end of the corridor had brought a sandwich
the sandwich contained an argument that had been left over from a previous conversation
previous conversations leave residue the way soup leaves the smell of itself in curtains
curtain smell is a technical term said the person eating the argument
eating an argument does not resolve it but it does make it quieter for a while
a while is a unit of time that refuses to be measured said the argument from inside the sandwich
the sandwich had opinions about many things but kept most of them to itself
keeping things to yourself is a form of storage that runs out without warning
without warning a kettle in the next room decided it had been a kettle long enough
long enough is different for kettles than for people but the principle is the same
the same principle applied to a hat that had spent three years on a shelf thinking about hats
thinking about hats while being a hat is a very specific kind of recursive despair
despair of this kind is usually accompanied by a faint whistling said the hat
the kettle agreed
agreement between objects is more common than people suppose
people suppose all kinds of things and most of them are somewhere between Tuesday and damp
damp is a mood as well as a condition said a wall that knew both very well
the wall had been damp for so long it had started to give advice to passing moisture
passing moisture said thank you which was unexpected
unexpected gratitude is the best kind because it arrives before you have prepared a face for it
a face for gratitude involves raising the upper portions of the face in a way that suggests surprise mixed with being pleased
being pleased and surprised at the same time is an efficient use of facial real estate said the wall
the moisture had already moved on which is the nature of moisture
nature is mostly just things moving on and occasionally becoming something else entirely
entirely different things sometimes turn out to be the same thing at different speeds
different speeds produce different kinds of Tuesday said the former compass who had overheard this
overhearing conclusions without the argument is a very modern form of understanding
modern understanding is mostly the feeling that you could probably understand it if you tried
trying is a corridor said the hat
the corridor was somewhere else at both ends and unavailable for comment
