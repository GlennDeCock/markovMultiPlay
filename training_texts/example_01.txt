the moon forgot its name and fell into a bucket of ordinary Tuesdays
the bucket had opinions about this
three sparrows carried a library between them none of the books were finished
a finished book is a closed argument said the leftmost sparrow
the rightmost sparrow had no mouth but continued anyway
somewhere a door opened into another door and behind that door was a helpful sign that said door
the helpful sign had been helpful for eleven years and was beginning to feel it
a man made entirely of borrowed hours walked into a shop that sold only the beginnings of sentences
he bought four and left before they could end
the floor of the shop was made of yesterday which is a very unreliable building material
Tuesday came back for its name but the moon had already given it to a fish
the fish did not want a name but accepted it politely
politeness is the shadow of a very small fear said no one in particular
in particular was a village two days walk from anywhere interesting
the interesting things had moved away years ago and sent postcards that arrived slightly before they were sent
a clock on the wall of in particular showed a time that had not happened yet
the clockmaker had made a small mistake or possibly a large one depending on the day
on the day in question a rabbit discovered that philosophy tasted like wet cardboard
this did not stop the rabbit from continuing
continuing is the main achievement of most things said the rabbit to the bucket
the bucket remembered the moon and felt briefly philosophical
briefly philosophical is a state most furniture passes through at least once
the chair in the corner had been philosophical for six months and now just felt tired
tiredness is a very long Tuesday said the chair to no one
no one agreed which is the most agreement no one ever gives
a woman walked backwards into the future holding a map of somewhere that no longer existed
the map was very detailed about the somewhere that no longer existed
it showed three bridges and a fountain and a shop that sold only the middles of conversations
she bought a middle and did not know what to do with the ends
the ends had gone home already and were making soup
soup made from the ends of conversations is very filling but hard to explain at dinner
at dinner a candle explained itself carefully to the darkness
the darkness took notes but lost them almost immediately
almost immediately is the speed of most important things said the candle before going out
going out is also a kind of arrival if you arrive somewhere darker
darkness has excellent acoustics for the sound of small decisions being made
the small decisions gathered in a corner and elected a representative
the representative was a slightly damp Tuesday that had been waiting for an opportunity
opportunity is a door that opens from the other side said the representative
on the other side a fish was carrying a name it did not want
the name was ordinary and Tuesday and belonged once to the moon
the moon had moved on and was now calling itself Gerald
Gerald did not suit the moon but the moon felt it was time for a change
a change is a beginning that forgot what it was changing from said Gerald the moon
the sparrows overheard this and wrote it down in the library they carried between them
the library was getting very heavy with things that had been overheard
overheard things weigh more than thought things because they have more witnesses
witnesses are just thoughts that happened outside said the leftmost sparrow
the rightmost sparrow nodded which is remarkable given the no mouth situation
between them the middle sparrow carried the card catalogue and said nothing
nothing is also a kind of something if you hold it long enough
long enough is different for everyone but usually involves a Tuesday
